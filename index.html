About Me

I am a novice web developer based in the Pacific Northwest. I am currently a student in the Web Development Certificate program at Seattle University.

I am a cultural anthropologist; I received my Master's degree in anthropology from Western Washington University in 2011. (Not that you would want to, but you can read my Master's thesis on the WWU Library website here.)

I am also a published scholar in the field of the scholarship of teaching and learning (SoTL) and I am a co-editor and co-author of the 2009 book Engaging Student Voices in the Study of Teaching and Learning from Stylus Publishing.
